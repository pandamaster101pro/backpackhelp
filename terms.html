2. What Backpackhelp Does

Backpackhelp helps students keep track of what's in their backpack. It connects to an RFID reader over Bluetooth (or remotely over Wi-Fi through a Raspberry Pi companion device) to detect tagged items and weigh your bag, and it can read a calendar feed you provide (such as Canvas) to build a daily packing checklist. You can also create packing checklists and set reminder notifications, which are stored and scheduled entirely on your device. Backpackhelp is an independent project, not the product of a registered company.
